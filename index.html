Incan Gold

Stumbling out of the jungle you see that you have finally discovered the five ancient temples of legend!

You stare in awe as a second figure emerges into the clearing, your archrival and fellow treasure hunter Dr. Jones!

Quickly, you must explore the temples and uncover the riches within, but be sure the perials of the ancient ruin don'tcause it to collapse with you still inside.

You and Dr. Jones rush into the first temple!

Game Rules

  • Incan Gold is played with a deck of 30 cards, 15 treasure cards (from 1-15 in value) and 15 hazard cards (5 types x 3 ea)
  • Treasure is split evenly between all adventurers inside the temple, any remainder is left behind and can be claimed by the first person to exit alone
  • If two hazard cards of the same type are played in the round, the temple will collapse, and players inside will lose the treasure they collected that round
  • After each new card players will have the choice to continue exploring the temple, or return to the surface and end their play for the round to secure what they have collected
  • Whichever player has the highest total score after 5 rounds wins!
