Game Rules

  • Rounds are played with a deck of 15 treasure cards (from 1-15 in value), 15 hazard cards (5 types x 3 ea) and an artifact.
  • Each turn all players will decide if they want to continue in search of treasure, or leave and secure what they have collected.
  • The value of a treasure card is evenly split between the players inside the temple, any remainder is left behind.
  • Remaining treasure is claimed if a single player chooses to exit the temple, if multiple players leave the value is lost.
  • If two hazard cards of the same type are played, the temple will collapse and any players still inside will lose the treasure they collect that round.
  • Artifacts can only be claimed if a single player chooses to leave on the following turn, scoring the entire value.
  • Whichever player has the highest total score after 5 rounds wins!
